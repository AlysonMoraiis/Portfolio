Welcome

Hello there! I'm Alyson, a passionate Game Developer based in São Paulo. I create games that challenge, entertain, and immerse players in unique experiences.

I run my solo studio, Traveler Studios, where I design, develop, and publish my own projects. Every day, I dedicate time to plan, prototype, and refine games that combine engaging mechanics, smooth gameplay, and thoughtful design. Along the way, I've participated in game jams and bootcamps, winning competitions and constantly pushing my creative and technical boundaries.

From childhood, games have been more than a hobby—they're my motivation. This love drives me to evolve as a developer, experiment with new ideas, and craft experiences that I hope players will truly enjoy. My goal is simple: make games I’m proud of and continue growing as a developer.
